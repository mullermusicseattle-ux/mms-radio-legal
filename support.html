Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe to Premium?

Open the app, tap the Premium option, and complete the purchase through the App Store. Your subscription unlocks ad-free, high-quality streaming.

How do I restore my subscription?

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, tap “Restore Purchases” to recover your active subscription.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone to view, change, or cancel.

The audio won't play.

Check your internet connection and make sure the app has permission to play audio. If problems persist, email us at the address above.
